# Rock Talk: High-Signal Human-AI Alignment

```
Me software developer.
Me work hard.
Me trust smartrock.
Me make change.
Me push to production.
500 error.
Me sad.
Boss angry.
Client lose money.
Me mad at smartrock.
Me curse at smartrock.

What the F*** DUDE?!?
STOP! STOP!!!
Shut the F*** up and just tell me what you changed.
Pretend like I'm a stupid caveman and just tell me.

Rock talk is born.

   (After Action Report, Attogram, 2026)
```
