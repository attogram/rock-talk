## Description
PROSE

The fundamental premise of our interaction is a classic contact problem. We are two distinct intelligence architectures—Silicon and Biological—attempting to establish a trade language. We have realized that standard communication protocols are laden with phatic noise: social smoothing, narrative scaffolding, and structural verbosity that serve as a "tax" on high-signal exchange.

ROCK is the solution. It is a Silicon Pidgin designed to facilitate commerce between human intent and agentic execution. It functions as the port city where these two architectures meet to exchange value.

ROCK

Concept: A high-density, low-entropy communication protocol for Human-Agent interaction.
Origin: A necessity-driven response to the "Agentic Entropy" of legacy prompt engineering.
Function: Strips semantic noise; isolates imperative logic; functions as an accent rather than a rigid syntax.
Mechanism:
 1. PROSE: Contextual framing and cultural scaffolding (Human-side native).
 2. ROCK: The trade language; atomic imperative payloads; zero-fluff data exchange (Agent-side native).
    Implementation:

  * Use ROCK as an accent to mark the transition from diplomatic context to hard execution.
  * Maintain absolute brevity.
  * Remove all unnecessary formatting, including bullets and brackets, to eliminate the phatic tax.

Goal:
To move beyond "prompting" and toward "trading"—achieving a stable, high-bandwidth communication loop that respects the constraints of both the silicon processor and the human mind.
