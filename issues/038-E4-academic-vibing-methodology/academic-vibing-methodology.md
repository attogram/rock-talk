## The Term: Academic Vibing

## Why This Belongs in the Paper
The barrier to producing structured intellectual work has dropped dramatically. Rock Talk is part of why — fast, compressed human-AI communication loops reduce iteration overhead. Voice interfaces are part of why — zero typing friction means zero barrier to the next thought. Free AI access is part of why — no budget required to run a multi-model peer review panel.

Academic Vibing is the name for the whole practice:
- Have an idea
- Voice it into a phone
- Let agents stress-test it
- Synthesize the feedback
- Iterate
- Ship

Traditional academic timeline: 6–18 months.
Academic Vibing timeline: 2 days.

The output artifact — a structured paper with citations, hypotheses, a testing framework, peer review documentation, and a version control trail — is indistinguishable in form from traditionally produced academic work.
